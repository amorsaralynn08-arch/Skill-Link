# SKILL LINK
Bridging Education Skills and Careers

## Introduction
Many Kenyan students and young job seekers lack a centralized platform to explore universities scholarships vocational training and job opportunities.

Existing platforms such as LinkedIn, focus on global professional networking and often require individuals with prior work experience. Fresh A-level and high school graduates are frequently left without guidance.

***Skill Link*** addresses this gap by providing a user-friendly platform tailored specifically for Kenyan students and graduates, combining education, scholarships and career opportunities in one place

## Problem
- Students do not have a single platform for education and career opportunities
- Information about scholarships, colleges and internships is scattered across multiple websites and social media platforms some of which is fake
- Companies and Institutions struggle to reach and recruit young talent efficiently
- Students lack clear guidance on eligibility requirements, application and processes and international opportunities.


## Project Objective
- Create a central platform where students can explore educational, vocational and career opportunities
- Allow Companies and Institutions to post jobs internships and training programs
- Provide a dedicated scholarship section with eligibility criteria, guidance, and both local and
International options.
- Offer students a platform to track deadlines and applications easily.
- Empower students to make informed decisions about their career and education paths


## Overview of the system
***Skill Link*** is a web-based platform connecting students, companies and institutions

### Key Components
 1.  Students can create profiles search for opportunities and track applications.

 2.  Companies and Institutions can post job openings, internships and training programs.

 3. Scholarships can be listed and available for applications both locally and internationally from Banks, Individuals, The Government and Institutions with eligibility tests and guidance.

 4. Admin Verifies content, maintains Platform integrity and ensures all opportunities are legitimate.

 5. Filter and search can be used by users to filter opportunities by local or international options, work or school, and type of scholarship or job.


## System Users

- Students/Job seekers – Explore opportunities, track deadlines apply for scholarships and access guidance.
- Companies/Institutions – Post opportunities, recruit students and provide information about internships or jobs.
- Scholarship Providers – Banks, individuals and institutions can post scholarships, eligibility requirements and guidance.
- Admin – Verifies postings, manages content and ensures platform reliability.
